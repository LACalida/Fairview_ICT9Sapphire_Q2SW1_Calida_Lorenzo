Chat Noir

Chat Noir is the alter ego of Adrien Agreste, a charming and kind-hearted teenage boy with a secret life as a superhero. With the help of his magical companion, Plagg, he transforms into the cat-themed hero Chat Noir to fight evil alongside his partner, Ladybug.
